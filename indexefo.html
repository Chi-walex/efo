how to make oha soup

oha soup which originates from Oha leaf, also scientifically known as Pterocarpus mildbraedii, is a popular vegetable native to the Igbos in Nigeria. “Oha” or “Ora” is the Igbo name while the English name is the African Rosewood plant.

ingredients

  1. oha leaf
  2. palm oil
  3. locust beans
  4. stock fish
  5. bell pepper
  6. smoked fish

steps to cook oha soup.

  • wash the oha leaf properly to remove foreign particles
  • place an empty cooking pot on the cooker and add palm oil
  • let it steam a bit , add some onions and locust beans and allow to steam
  • after 15 minutes pour the smoked and stock fish
  • then, add the leaves and allow to cook
  • in 15 - 20 minutes oha is ready
